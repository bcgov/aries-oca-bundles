# City of Vancouver DEV Municipal Services Digital Letter of Authorization

A schema for a "Municipal Services Digital Letter of Authorization (MSDiLOA)" issued by the City of Vancouver. The MSDiLOA represents a digital authorization for a person to apply for or access municipal services on behalf of another person or a business.

- Publishing Organization: City Of Vancouver
